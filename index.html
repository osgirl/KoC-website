Welcome to Archbishop
Thomas J. Murphy Council 12251

We're located at St. Cecilia Parish on Bainbridge Island, Washington. The current Grand Knight is Dick Hassell. You can contact the Knights by phone at 206-842-3594, or by email at courtneylaw@comcast.net.

Council Meetings

We meet the first Saturday of each month in Conger Hall after the 9am Mass and Rosary.

About Our Council

Archbishop Thomas J. Murphy Council was founded in 1997 through the efforts of parishoners Dick O'Brien, Anthony Farrell and others. It is named after Archbishop Thomas J. Murphy, who was archbishop of the Seattle archdiocese from 1991 to 1997. The Thomas J. Murphy Council works closely with St. Cecilia's Parish on Bainbridge Island, raising funds for student scholarships, youth activities and other charitable causes.

Working with our pastor, Fr. Joseph Mitchell, the Knights support the mission of St. Cecilia Parish as well as upholding the tradition of the Knights of Columbus to support seminarians, donate to charities and strengthen the culture of life.
